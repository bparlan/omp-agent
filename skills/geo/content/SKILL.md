# Content E-E-A-T Assessment

## E-E-A-T Signals

| Signal | What to Look For |
|--------|-----------------|
| Expertise | Author credentials, author bios, expertise claims |
| Experience | First-hand experience, original data, case studies |
| Authoritativeness | Citations, authority links, industry recognition |
| Trustworthiness | Contact info, terms of service, clear ownership |

## Assessment Checklist

- [ ] Author bios with credentials
- [ ] Publication dates on content
- [ ] Source citations for claims
- [ ] "About" page quality
- [ ] Contact information accessibility
- [ ] Content freshness (updated within 1 year)
